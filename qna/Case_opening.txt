Q(1):What do you need help with?
A: How do I open a case?
	Q(1.1):First you need to find the Case Opening Form. You can find this in your clinic's S drive or where your clinic office keeps blank forms. It looks like this: <a href="https://suffolklitlab.org/clio/images/caseopen.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://suffolklitlab.org/clio/images/caseopen.png"/></a>
	A:Ok, I found it, now what?
		Q(1.1.1):Now you fill it out with as much information as you can. Don't forget the conflict check!
		A:How do I run a conflict check?
		A:I ran a conflict check and filled out the form, now what?
			Q(1.1.1.2):Now you should take it to your supervisor to have it signed. 
			A:My supervisor signed it, now what?
				Q(1.1.1.2.1):You need to make a copy of the signed form. You will keep the original in your file.
				A:What do I do with the copy?
					Q(1.1.1.2.1.1):You have to take the copy to the office's administrative assistant and they will enter this information in Clio for you.
					A:Now what?
						Q(1.1.1.2.1.1.1):Once the case is open you can start adding information to the matter!<br><br>Don't forget to scan the case opening form into Clio and save it in the matter under Documents! 
						A:Thanks!
