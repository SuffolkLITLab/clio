Click on the blue “New” button that is located on the right side of the page.

Select “Document from Template” from the drop-down menu.
A window titled “Create A Document” will appear. In the first box, select the template you wish to use for your document.



In the second box, select the matter that this document will be taking information from.
Next, you can select how you want the document to be saved (PDF and/or Word document).

Once this information has been filled in, click on “Create Document”.
You will then be required to refresh the page to view the document as listed.

The document will appear in the matter folder. If the template was categorized, however, then under the “category” column, you will see in which folder the document was placed in.

Click on the “Calendar” tab on the left.

On top of the calendar, click on the green “Add Event” button.

A window titled “Create Calendar Entry” will open. Here you can enter information concerning the event that you wish to submit.
Under “Matter”, select the case you wish to apply this event to. You may also add a location and invite others by selecting “Invite contacts Via Clio Connect”.

On top, you will see tabs “Repeats”, “Reminders” and “Time Entries”.

If you click on the “Repeats” tab, you can control how often the event will repeat and notify you of its occurrence.

If you click on the “Reminders” tab, you can control when you are alerted of the event for the date it starts and whether to be alerted by an email or pop-up notification.
Once you have filled in all the necessary information, you may click “Create Calendar Entry” to finish or “ Save and Add Another” to create an additional calendar entry.
