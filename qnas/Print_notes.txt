Q(1):Printing notes for a matter should only take about a minute. Navigate to the matter you want to print notes for. Click on the <i>Notes</i> tab.
At the bottom of the page you will find en 'Export' button. Click it.
<br><br><a href="https://suffolklitlab.org/clio/images/print.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://suffolklitlab.org/clio/images/print.png"/></a><br><br>
You should see an option to export in 'PDF' or 'CSV' format. Select 'PDF.'
<br><br><a href="https://suffolklitlab.org/clio/images/export_notes.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://suffolklitlab.org/clio/images/export_notes.png"/></a><br><br>
