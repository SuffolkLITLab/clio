Q(1):Printing notes from Clio is easy and it should only take 1 minute!<br><br>Once you are logged into Clio go to the matter you want to print notes from.
A:I'm there, now what?
	Q(1.1):Now go to the Notes tab.
	A:Ok, what do I do now?
		Q(1.1.1):At the bottom of the page you will find en 'Export' button. Click on it. <p><a href="https://suffolklitlab.org/clio/images/print.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://suffolklitlab.org/clio/images/print.png"/></a></p>
		A:I clicked on it, what do I do now?
			Q(1.1.1.1):You should see an option to export in 'PDF' or 'CSV' format. Select 'PDF' <p><a href="https://suffolklitlab.org/clio/images/export_notes.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://suffolklitlab.org/clio/images/export_notes.png"/></a></p>
			A:Done, now what?
				Q(1.1.1.1.1):A PDF should have downloaded. When you open it you will be able to see all your notes on that matter and print the PDF.
				A:That was easy, thanks!
