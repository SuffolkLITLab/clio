Q(1): To have a staff member open your case in Clio, you need to fill out a <a href="https://suffolklitlab.org/clio/docs/case_opening_form.pdf" target="_blank">Case Opening Form</a>. You can find this in your clinic's S drive or where your clinic keeps blank forms. It looks like this: 
<br><br><a href="https://suffolklitlab.org/clio/images/caseopen.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://suffolklitlab.org/clio/images/caseopen.png" style="width:232px;height:177px"/></a><br><br>
Once you've filled out as much as you can (don't forget the conflict check), take it to your supervisor to have it signed.<br><br>
Make a copy of the signed form (keep the original in your file).
Take the copy to your office's administrative assistant and they will enter this information in Clio for you.
Once the case is open, you can start adding information to the matter.<br><br>
Don't forget to scan the case opening form into Clio, and save it to the matter under Documents.
