Q(1): Using Clio Launcher, you can edit common document types (e.g., Word) without first having to manually downloading them. Just click on the blue arrow to the right of the document. <br><br>
<a href="https://suffolklitlab.org/clio/images/launcher_arrow.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://suffolklitlab.org/clio/images/launcher_arrow.png" style="width:232px;"/></a><br><br>
The first time you use Clio Luncher you may be asked to sign in with your Clio credentials and connect your accounts. Clicking on the blue arrow should open the document in a native editor (e.g., Word) though it can take a while to open. You can then edit the document, and hit save as you normally would. This will save the document back to Clio, requiring no additional action on your part. <br><br>
You can check to make sure your changes have been saved by looking at the document's version history.<br><br>
<a href="https://suffolklitlab.org/clio/images/doc_details.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://suffolklitlab.org/clio/images/doc_details.png" style="width:232px;"/></a><br><br>
You can also download old versions of the document from here as well. <br><br>

Q(2): Hi! What do you need help with?
A: I would like to know how to edit a document in clio.
	Q(2.1): Understood. First, find the document you wish to edit by typing in the client's name in the search bar. Click on the matter that has the document you wish to edit. <br><br><a href="https://suffolklitlab.org/clio/images/edit_documents1.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://suffolklitlab.org/clio/images/edit_documents1.png" style="width:232px;"/></a> Alternatively, you can click on the documents tab on the left bar and choose your document there.
	A: Okay, I did that. Now what?
		Q(2.1.1): If you are searching for your document through the matter, click on the white documents tab at the top. Then at the bottom, choose your document you wish to edit. <br><br><a href="https://suffolklitlab.org/clio/images/edit_documents2.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://suffolklitlab.org/clio/images/edit_documents2.png" style="width:232px;"/></a>
		A: I have chosen my document. What do I do now?
			Q(2.1.1.1): Click the document name to download the document. Once it is downloaded, you may edit and save any changes you make to the document. Please note that if the document is a pdf, you may need to have adobe software to make changes.
